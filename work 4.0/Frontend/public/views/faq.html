Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is PerfectDhaga?

PerfectDhaga is an eCommerce platform that connects users with skilled tailors and vendors for custom clothing.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by signing up on the registration page. Fill in your details and choose your role (user, tailor, vendor).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and secure payment gateways.

Can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your order in the 'Order Tracking' section.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping fees and delivery times vary based on location and will be calculated at checkout.
